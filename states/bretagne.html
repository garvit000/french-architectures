Bretagne

Château de Josselin

This 13th-century Gothic cathedral captivates with its towering spires, which reach high above the Quimper skyline, and its magnificent stained-glass windows that cast vibrant colors into the interior. The cathedral’s finely crafted arches and vaulted ceilings create a sense of awe, highlighting the skill of medieval craftsmen. These two sites together represent the best of Brittany’s architectural beauty and historical depth, drawing visitors from around the world to admire their timeless grandeur.
