La Cité du Vin in Bordeaux

La Cité du Vin in Bordeaux is a unique cultural landmark dedicated to the world of wine. Opened in 2016, this impressive building reflects the shapes and movements of wine in a glass through its curved, flowing design. Created by architects Anouk Legendre and Nicolas Desmazières, the structure combines glass and metal to create a sense of fluidity. Inside, visitors experience interactive exhibits, workshops, and a panoramic tasting room that offers views over Bordeaux and the Garonne River. The architecture of La Cité du Vin symbolizes the blending of tradition and innovation in wine culture, making it a must-visit for wine enthusiasts and architecture lovers alike.
